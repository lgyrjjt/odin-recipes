Salad



A salad is really nice if you like heathly things. You can add what you want to it so it is not just rabbit food.

What you put in a salad is up to the chef. It takes years of training to put the leaves in a bowl and add other coloured vegtables

Ingredients

  • Green stuff - maybe lettace, maybe lettace, maybe lettace
  • Might as well add some cucumber
  • Carrots are nice
  • Peppers
  • Grapes
  • Fig leaves
  • Herbs

Steps

  1. Lay out the lettace really nicely
  2. Chop the cucumber into uneven bits
  3. Grate carrot
  4. Cut grapes and herbs
  5. Throw it in a bowl
