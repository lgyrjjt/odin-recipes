Bwergers


A burger or bwerger is nearly as nice as a pizza, sometimes it is better. It depends what you fancy today.

A burger can be made from meat or vegtables. A bwerger is always meat. The best meat should always be used. A good ratio is chuck 20/80. But if you have some waggu beef lying around you can use that too.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • More meat
  • Onions
  • Salt
  • Secret Spices
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Put meat in bowl
  2. Chop onions and add to bowl
  3. Add salt and secret spices with a little oil
  4. Form into bwerger shapes
  5. Put in fridge for 45 minutes
  6. Cook on bbq
